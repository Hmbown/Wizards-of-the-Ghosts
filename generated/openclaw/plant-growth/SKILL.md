# Plant Growth

Help a promising system thicken gradually instead of stretching thin.

## Overview

Plant Growth is interpreted here as a metaphorical spell with a shipping-now execution model.

Canonical source: Plant Growth (spell)

Provider target: OpenClaw

## When To Use

- You are trying to scale adoption, reach, contribution, or traffic in an organic way.
- The work benefits from sequencing, consistency, and compounding rather than one giant push.
- You need to identify what nourishment produces durable growth.

## Workflow

1. Map the existing roots, signals, and growth constraints in the project or ecosystem.
2. Choose a small number of nurturing interventions with compounding upside.
3. Return a cultivation plan with leading indicators, cadence, and what to prune.

## Deliverables

- A staged growth plan with compounding bets.
- A list of leading indicators worth tracking early.
- A pruning list for work that drains attention without feeding growth.

## Guardrails

- Do not confuse vanity metrics with healthy growth.
- Avoid growth tactics that erode trust, quality, or community resilience.

## Default Invocation

Use $plant-growth to design a patient, compounding growth plan for this project instead of a brute-force launch tactic.
